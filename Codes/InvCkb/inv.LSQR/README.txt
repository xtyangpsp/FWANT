Conjugate Gradient Routine
N. Trupin 2024
=========================

to make: `make`
to make and run: `make run`
to run: `sh run.sh`
to submit to slurm: `sbatch run.sh`
to profile with massif: `valgrind --tool="massif" --log-file="[file_name]" [exe_name]`
to profile with callgrind: `valgrind --tool="callgrind" --log-file="[file_name]" [exe_name]`

output to terminal by default. pipe to file with `[run_command] > [output_file]
